## üìù Policy Template Structure

### **Standard Policy Format:**
```
1. Purpose
2. Scope
3. Definitions
4. Policy Statement
5. Procedures
6. Responsibilities
7. Compliance
8. Review and Updates
9. Contact Information
```

### **Required Elements:**
- **Clear Purpose** - Why the policy exists
- **Defined Scope** - Who and what it covers
- **Specific Procedures** - How to implement
- **Clear Responsibilities** - Who does what
- **Compliance Measures** - How to ensure compliance
- **Review Schedule** - When to update
- **Contact Information** - Who to contact
