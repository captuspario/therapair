## üõ°Ô∏è Business Protection Strategy

### **1. Intellectual Property Protection**

#### **Core IP Assets:**
- **Matching Algorithm:** Proprietary AI/ML matching technology
- **Brand & Trademarks:** "Therapair" name, logo, taglines
- **Software & Code:** Platform, widget, matching engine
- **Data Assets:** Anonymized matching patterns, user insights
- **Trade Secrets:** Algorithm parameters, business processes

#### **Protection Mechanisms:**
- **Patents:** File provisional patents for matching algorithm
- **Trademarks:** Register "Therapair" and key brand elements
- **Copyrights:** Protect software code and content
- **Trade Secrets:** Implement confidentiality agreements
- **NDAs:** All employees, contractors, and partners

### **2. Legal Structure Protection**

#### **Corporate Governance:**
- **Board Control:** Prequel maintains majority board seats
- **Decision Rights:** Prequel controls strategic decisions
- **Veto Rights:** Unison has veto on mental health ethics decisions
- **Exit Rights:** Clear exit mechanisms for both parties

#### **Liability Protection:**
- **Limited Liability:** Pty Ltd structure limits personal liability
- **Professional Indemnity:** Insurance for mental health services
- **Directors & Officers:** D&O insurance for board members
- **Cyber Liability:** Insurance for data breaches

### **3. Financial Protection**

#### **Revenue Protection:**
- **Diversified Revenue:** Multiple revenue streams reduce risk
- **Recurring Revenue:** Subscription model provides stability
- **Cash Reserves:** Maintain 6-month operating runway
- **Debt Management:** Minimize debt, maintain creditworthiness

#### **Investment Protection:**
- **Valuation Control:** Prequel controls valuation decisions
- **Dilution Protection:** Anti-dilution provisions for Prequel
- **Exit Strategy:** Clear exit mechanisms and valuation methods

## üìä Ownership & Equity Structure

### **Proposed Equity Split:**
- **Prequel:** 65% (Majority control, strategic direction)
- **Unison Mental Health:** 35% (Strategic partner, clinical expertise)
- **Employee Stock Option Pool:** 10-15% (Future team incentives)
- **Future Investors:** Dilution from employee pool and future rounds

### **Voting Rights:**
- **Prequel:** 65% voting control
- **Unison:** 35% voting rights
- **Special Voting:** Unison veto on clinical/ethical decisions
- **Super Majority:** 75% required for major decisions (acquisition, dissolution)

### **Economic Rights:**
- **Dividends:** Proportional to ownership percentage
- **Liquidation:** Proportional distribution of assets
- **Tag-Along Rights:** Unison can sell with Prequel
- **Drag-Along Rights:** Prequel can force Unison to sell (with protections)
